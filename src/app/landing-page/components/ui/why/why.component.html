Why we started Atrib

Digital marketing attribution is often biased, offline marketing attribution is difficult and privacy concerns are increasingly limiting user tracking. As a result, large parts of SMEs marketing spend is wasted.

There are advanced tools to alleviate these challenges, but they are normally reserved for large corporations and global brands. We want to level the playing field for SMEs, by democratising these tools.

Our mission is to help small businesses spend their hard-earned marketing money wisely.

How Atrib Disrupts

We have taken an advanced analytical process called Marketing Mix Modelling (MMM) and digitised plus automated it, so that pretty much any marketing driven business can have access to it.

MMM is usually performed by expensive marketing agencies – using data scientists or other highly paid specialists – to crunch data and conclude how much a marketing channel actually contributes to the sales of a business. As a result, the agencies can suggest or help simulate how changes in future channel mix or spend would improve marketing performance.

This is essentially what Atrib Pro does, just much smarter and for a fraction of the cost.

What Atrib also can do

We have built what is probably the best visualisation platform for marketing data out there. We have also added smooth connections with most major digital marketing channels, so that our users can easily fetch and view their own data. On top of this, we have added clever layers of publicly available data, to show how businesses are impacted by external factors, providing an eye-opening view on any marketing driven SME.

We offer all this entirely for free.
